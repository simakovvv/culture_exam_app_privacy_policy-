# Privacy Policy for Hungarian Culture Exam App

**Last updated: 16.11.2025**

## 1. General Information

Hungarian Culture Exam ("the App") is an educational application that helps users prepare for the Hungarian Cultural Knowledge Exam.
The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.

The App is designed for general educational use and does not require user registration or an account.

## 2. Subscriptions and Payments

The App offers an optional auto-renewable subscription.
All payments, refunds, and billing operations are processed exclusively through Google Play in accordance with the Google Play Terms of Service.

We do not have access to:

- your name
- your email address
- your Google account
- your payment information
- your Google Play purchase history

Google Play provides us only with a purchase token, which is used internally to unlock premium content on your device.

## 3. Data Collection

The App:

- does NOT collect personal data
- does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- does NOT store data on external servers
- does NOT request permissions beyond those required for app functionality
- processes all study progress and settings locally on the user's device

## 4. Sharing of Data

Since the App does not collect data, it does not share anything with third parties.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13+.
Since the App does not collect any personal information, it is compliant with COPPA/GDPR-K requirements.

## 6. Security

All data (if any) remains exclusively on the user's device.
No external databases or cloud servers are used.

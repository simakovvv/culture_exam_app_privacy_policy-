# Privacy Policy for Hungarian Culture Exam App

**Effective Date:** December 22, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 22, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Hungarian Culture Exam mobile application ("App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services. The App is an educational application designed to help users prepare for the Hungarian Cultural Knowledge Exam.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

The App does not require user registration or account creation. We do not collect personal identification information such as name, email address, or phone number.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Analytics Data**: We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect anonymous usage data to improve the App. This includes:

- Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
- Usage data (features accessed, screens viewed, session duration, app interactions)
- Technical data (app version, language preference, crash reports)
- Aggregate statistics (number of days since installation for cohort analysis)

**Local Storage**: The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- Study progress and learning history
- User preferences and settings
- Dictionary lookup history

This locally stored data is never transmitted to our servers.

### 1.3 Subscription and Payment Information

When you purchase a subscription through Google Play:

- We receive an anonymous purchase token from Google Play
- We generate and store a random unique identifier (UUID) on your device
- We transmit this UUID and purchase token to our backend server solely for subscription verification

We do NOT have access to:
- Your payment card details
- Your Google account information
- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your purchase history beyond the current subscription status

All payment processing is handled exclusively by Google Play in accordance with their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Local Data**: Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data

## 6. Children's Privacy

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: All data transmissions between the App and our servers use industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
